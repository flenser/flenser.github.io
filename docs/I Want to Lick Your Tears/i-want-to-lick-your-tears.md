## Original Title

かわいい君の涙を舐めたい。睨まれたい。

## Synopsis

### Thud, thud. I am being corrupted. I dream

---

Houu-chan: model student, pretty, socially gifted, universally beloved.\
Her smile is adorable, just like a dog's. Once, just once, I ruffled and kneaded her cheeks with both hands. The look on her face afterwards, her whole attitude, as if to say *do it more* — so comfortable, so willing. Quite endearing.\
I find Houu-chan endearing.\
I have come to find her endearing.

She’s come to think I find her endearing.

Smarter than Houu-chan, yet a problem student. No charm to speak of, socially inept, treated like a leper. Bad at smiling, though I can manage a sullen expression just fine. That someone like her should have taken a liking to me.

*How pitiful.*

Poor Houu-chan.

You see, I do love that adorable smile of yours.\
But it's precisely because I love it that I find myself wanting, so desperately, to see something else.\
Your face when you cry. And then, that face consumed entirely by fury.

*………It makes me shiver.*

And beyond that, well. If I'm allowed to want more, then:

From you, so guileless and carefree — contempt, scorn, anything will do.\
For now, just with those cold, cold eyes of yours that make me feel like I'm about to be abandoned.

*I want you to glare at me.*
