**Episode 14: On a Night Like Foam**

...Excuse me?

I tore my eyes away from Yuno on reflex and turned to face the mirror.\
Rinsing out the shampoo foam under the shower, my head was very busy.

Eh, why.\
Hold on a moment.\
What am I supposed to do here.

"Why are you coming in here?"\

"That's what happens when you watch things like that."\

"Regardless, don't come in without asking."\

"Hey, big sis."

"Wai — wa-wa, wait——"\
"Hm?"

"That part... is... I'll wash that myself."

Hopelessly flustered, even to my own ears, I moved my own hands to take over. Being watched even while I did that was embarrassing enough.
