## Original Title

エリート女社長が路地裏で拾った年下女にひたすら甘やかされて沼っていく百合の話

## Description

### Out in the world she's the perfect older woman, but only in front of me she's adorable, and it makes me shiver

\
Seto Reina is a company president who never shows weakness to anyone. One rainy night, she picks up a woman alone in an alley.

A woman with an unnervingly beautiful face, who seems innocent but carries something precarious about her — Yuno.

It was only meant to be a temporary arrangement, until Yuno found a new place to live. At first, Reina thought of herself as keeping Yuno, like a pet.

But by the time she noticed, it was herself who was locked inside the cage.

A perverse yuri story in which a perfect, cold woman is spoiled and slowly dissolves, sunk by the girl she picked up in an alley.
