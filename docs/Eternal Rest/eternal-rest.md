## Original Title

疲れたから永眠

## Description

### I Have Fallen from Grace

The Lord said: "If you are tired, then rest."\
And so I fell.\
To a pitiful girl who wished to be at ease — I handed her a ticket to Heaven, and borrowed it.\
Her body.

Now then. I have fallen from grace.\
In the human world, I intend to cast aside my duties as an angel and live freely, at my leisure.

………

Oh my?

In this world — there is something.\
Neither angel nor demon. Not human either.\
Something appears to be here.

"Shall we search for them together, Komamori-san? The inhuman apparitions that cause mischief."
