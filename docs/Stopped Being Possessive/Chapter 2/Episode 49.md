**Episode 49: By My Side — Shiori's Side**

\
*side.Shiori*

In the quiet room, the steady sound of sleeping breath.\
Leaning against the arm of the sofa, I gaze at Yuna-chan's sleeping face.

The faint shadows under her eyes, that exhausted, slackened expression — she must have been pushing herself, this whole time.\
Watching Yuna-chan lately, that much was obvious at a glance.

Because of always being with Shiraishi-san.\
Because that person wouldn't let go of her.

But that relationship is over now.

—More precisely: I ended it.

I slowly run my fingers through Yuna's hair.\
Feeling it flow smooth and fine through my fingers, I let my eyes narrow, just slightly.

"I've made it this far..."

Yuna-chan had only ever had eyes for Shiraishi-san, this whole time.\
No matter how close I got, no matter how much attention I paid her — even so, there was only Shiraishi-san in her heart.

But now it's different.

Shiraishi-san and Yuna-chan aren't lovers anymore.

Neither of them ever clearly said the words "we're breaking up."\
But in practical terms, it amounted to the same thing.

Yuna-chan left Shiraishi-san's side.\
And now, she's sleeping here, in my house, close beside me.

—Once it's come this far, it's fine now.

I narrow my eyes quietly, combing my fingers through Yuna-chan's hair.\
Fine and soft, the texture strokes gently against my fingertips.

Until now, the one who stood beside her was Shiraishi-san.\
But that's already a thing of the past.

"Yuna-chan, you don't need Shiraishi-san anymore."

I murmur it like a whisper, and let my gaze fall to her hand.\
Slender, fragile-looking.\
Whoever's it might have been until now — soon, this hand will be mine.

...Because Yuna-chan shouldn't be in Shiraishi-san's arms anymore.

I can be gentler than that.\
I can properly put her at ease.

So the rest is just a matter of time.

She's left Shiraishi-san's side now.\
Hurt, exhausted, looking for somewhere to lean on.\
I can be that place.

I gently touch her cheek.\
Soft, and slightly cool skin.\
Right now, she's simply sleeping peacefully.

Being able to touch her like this fills me with an unbearable happiness.\
I finally feel it — that I've made it this far, to this distance.

...So, please, don't hesitate anymore.

Just choose me, instead of someone like Shiraishi-san.\
I can treasure Yuna-chan far more than Shiraishi-san ever could.\
I can stay by her side, always.

Wherever Yuna-chan wants to go, I'll go there with her.\
I'll support her more than anyone, love her more than anyone, treasure her more than anyone.

Yuna-chan should already be mine.\
If things go on like this, her heart drawing closer to me little by little — yes, surely—

—And yet.

And yet, just the smallest amount of anxiety still remains.

What if.

What if Yuna-chan still says she wants to go back to Shiraishi-san, after all this.

I—

I—I—

*(...What am I supposed to do?)*

Gripping my faintly trembling fingers tight, I let out a quiet breath.
