**Episode 1: Ordinary Days**

\
The moment the bell rang, I — Nanase Yuna — was already on my feet.\
I knew exactly where I was headed.\
There was no reason for me to stay in my own classroom.

The place I belonged was beside my beloved girlfriend, Shiraishi Honoka.

I hurried out of my classroom and down the hall. Class 2 was only a few steps away. Even that short distance felt unbearably long.

I stood at the door and threw it open.\
My eyes immediately swept the room, searching for her.

Sure enough — surrounded by a few classmates again today.

*A familiar sight.*

Someone would talk to Honoka, they'd laugh together, and every time, something in my chest churned.\
I walked straight ahead. I wasn't going to waste effort dodging people. I went directly to Honoka.

On the way, I locked eyes with a boy who'd been approaching her.

I stared him down.

He flinched, looked away, and left.

That wasn't all.

The other classmates around Honoka noticed me too, and one by one, exchanging glances, they started drifting away.

I didn't say a word. I just looked.\
That was enough.

Before I knew it, only I was left standing beside Honoka.

"Yuna... did you glare at someone again?"

Honoka looked at me, a little troubled.

"...Well, he was getting too close."\
"He was just talking to me."\
"Still didn't like it."

I pouted. Honoka sighed, but smiled through it, and patted my head.

"Honestly... you're hopeless."

The gesture loosened something in my chest, just a little.\
But it didn't make the possessiveness go away.

"Honoka, say ahh."

I took the bread from her hands and tore off a bite-sized piece, lifting it to her mouth.

"Eh... here, of all places?"\
"Not allowed?"

I stared at her, and she opened her mouth, just slightly, looking a little embarrassed.

"...Ahh."

She ate the piece from my hand. The gesture was so cute my cheeks went slack on their own.

"Honoka's so cute."\
"Geez... everyone's watching."\
"Doesn't matter. I want them to see that Honoka's mine."

I said it while quietly taking her hand. Her cheeks colored a little.

"Yuna, you really are possessive."\
"Of course I am. Honoka's mine."

I leaned my head onto her shoulder. She looked a little flustered but didn't push me away.

"Geez... fine, I give up."

She stroked my head gently. I closed my eyes, relaxing into the warmth of her hand.

*— If only Honoka would always look only at me, forever like this.*

A dozen or so minutes later, the bell rang again.

"We should head back, Yuna."\
"...Don't wanna."\
"Class is about to start."

Honoka laughed and gave my arm a light tap.\
The gesture was so cute it made me pout a little more.

"I'll come get you after school."\
"Okay, got it."

Holding back the urge to keep her smile all to myself, I dragged my feet back to my own classroom.\
My steps were heavy. With every step down the hallway, Honoka's warmth felt further away, and I kept almost turning back.

Still, I somehow made it back to class, where my classmate Takamiya Shiori was watching me.

"Welcome back, Nanase-san. Off to see Shiraishi-san again today?"

She said it with a gentle smile.

"...What."\
"Nothing. I just think your relationship is really lovely."\
Shiori sat down quietly in the seat beside mine, still smiling.

"You always go see Shiraishi-san, don't you. I envy that — being able to love someone that single-mindedly."

"It's nothing, really..."

I mumbled something vague, pulling out my chair and sitting down. I propped my chin on my hand and looked away. Shiori didn't seem to mind, resting her own chin on the desk, still watching me.

"Fufu. You really do love Shiraishi-san, don't you."

Just small talk. Nothing behind it — just an ordinary classmate's remark.

I gave a noncommittal hum and turned to look out the window. Shiori still seemed like she wanted to say more, but I had no interest, so I ignored her.

Still, she kept talking to me anyway — strange person.

With that thought lingering, I waited for class to begin.
