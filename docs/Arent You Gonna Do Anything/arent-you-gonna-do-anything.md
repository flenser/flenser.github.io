## Original Title

「なぁ、何もしてくれへんの？」って年上同期に言われる百合【完結】

## Synopsis

### If someone invites you, surely you’d touch them a bit? —Well, I don’t!

---

Kishimoto Mio is a plain-Jane second-year office worker at an anime production company.\
One night, she misses the last train and ends up walking home a drunk older colleague — the "sloppy little devil" Ashida Riwa — and staying the night.\
Riwa's sense of personal space is all wrong, but she's oddly gentle, and Mio doesn't know what to make of her.

*"Hey, Mio-chan… aren't you gonna do anything?"*

A voice in the dark, under the covers.\
Is she joking? Does she mean it? Mio can't tell. But her heart and her body are already answering —

A workplace yuri about a self-doubting pushover and a slightly sloppy older devil-woman, sharing one futon for a night that might just be once-in-a-lifetime.
