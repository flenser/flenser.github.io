## I Dedicate This Final Note to You.mp3

\
**I Dedicate This Final Note to You**\
*Lyrics: Ogawa Uta*\
*Composition: Kanzaki Shion*

---

I found it — my ideal\
Before I knew it, I was reaching out\
Passing on\
The words that existed to meet you

Trembling eyes\
The sound of a bell\
Silver-white brightness, glittering like stars\
Dazzling, shining

That name\
And the same colour\
Dyeing me, slowly

I want to be your reason to play\
Words that let you take flight

I want to protect —\
What binds those fingertips\
Let me change it into a promise

The blue of spring, the summer I touched\
Even now, all of it\
Together with the future, I'll weave it into words\
These days, this tenderness —\
I won't make them into memories\
Because we'll always be together

I'm lonely —\
But don't look back\
I'm watching the sound of your flight\
Writing the beautiful ideal\
Reaching out\
Until the day it arrives

I love you —\
From when it began\
My reason is you

I love you —\
Until when it ends\
I dedicate my words to your music

*lalala~*

Let's look at the sea from the same place\
Holding hands
