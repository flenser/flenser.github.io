## Original Title

義姉妹予定の幼馴染への片思いは、ご都合主義過ぎる！

## Synopsis

### You Let Us Live Together, and Now You're Telling Me I Can't Date Her?! (Furious)

---

I, Sukue Yotsuka, have a childhood friend.
Our houses are next door to each other. We're the same age — down to the birthday, even. We've been in the same class forever, sitting near each other the whole time.
The girl born from that suspiciously convenient prank of god's: Uzuide Makina.
Good-looking, good personality, and on top of that, her sense of personal space around me is physically broken. Let me just say this up front — of course I was going to fall for Makina. Of course I was!!

But then our parents go and remarry each other, and suddenly she's supposed to become my stepsister?!
Closer than physically close — family-register close?!
And on top of that, we're starting a "trial cohabitation to become a family" together?!

"Yotsuka... should we take a bath together?"

Huh?! Are you kidding me, god?!
You set me up with the perfect situation, and now, after all this, why does she have to become my family?! Why, why— (continued in the tagline)

"—There's no way this is normal!!"
