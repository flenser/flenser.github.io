## Synopsis

### No home. No money. No social skills. But an SSR-tier face.

Nanami Riri is an introverted beauty with zero communication skills, feeding her hunger for validation one selfie at a time on the internet.\
Then, one day, the company her parents ran goes bankrupt, her parents skip town, and Riri finds herself broke and homeless.

The one who takes her in is Kurusu Akira, a blonde gal who runs a maid café.\
Living under Akira's roof, Riri — despite her social anxiety — starts working part-time at the café just to get by...

An older gal, a childhood friend from her old school (a young lady of means), a landmine-type tsundere, a gentle, easygoing onee-san... and even the café's customers, apparently!?

"Seriously, you've got such a cute face." (the gal)\
"Riri likes me best, right?" (the childhood friend)\
"...Well, I don't hate you or anything." (the landmine tsundere)\
"Fufu, Riri-chan, you're just like a little doll, aren't you~ I want to keep you as a pet." (the gentle onee-san)\
"Please, let me spend my entire paycheck on you." (a corporate slave regular customer, at the end of her rope)

However you look at it, "pretty-faced" Riri finds herself loved by more and more people before she even realizes what's happening!?

"Fufu, hehe... well, I mean, I do have a cute face, and it's not like being wanted feels bad — actually, it feels pretty great, if I'm honest..."

And with Riri's attitude like that, everyone's affection for her only heats up further!?

A national-treasure-tier introverted beauty grows through her part-time job while being loved by everyone around her!\
A chaotic yuri rom-com overflowing with beautiful girls!
