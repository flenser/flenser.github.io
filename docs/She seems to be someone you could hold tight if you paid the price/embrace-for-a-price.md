## Original Title

無表情で透明な彼女はお金さえ払えば抱き潰してもいいらしい

## Description

Mashiro has been watching the beautiful Amamiya for a long time; Amamiya, the class beauty with the reputation for being easy when she gets paid.

One fateful day their eyes meet and Amamiya approaches Mashiro with 2 questions: “You like me, don’t you?” and “Hey, Mashiro-chan. Won’t you treat me?”.

Thus begins a complicated relationship.
