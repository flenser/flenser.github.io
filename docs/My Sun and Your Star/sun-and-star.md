## Original Title

私の太陽とあなたの星

## Description

When I tried to kill myself, the prettiest girl in my year saved me and now I'm tutoring her?!

Takisawa Sora, utterly fed up with her circumstances and life, considers suicide on the school rooftop. However, she is spotted and stopped...
The one who stopped her was Endo Hina, the beautiful and popular girl in her year.

After Takisawa was stopped from ending her life by Endo, whose life seemed perfect, the situation leads them to start studying together...?

The sweet yet bittersweet romance between the prettiest girl in the year and the top student in the year.

Watch as the girl who wanted to die and the girl living with her own burdens become entangled, their feelings gradually shifting.
