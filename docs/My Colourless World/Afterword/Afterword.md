## *To You Who Read to the End*

\
To everyone who read *You Bleed Into My Colourless World* all the way to the end — thank you, truly.

I hope you'll forgive me for imitating professional authors with an afterword of my own, but I wanted to find a way to express my gratitude to those of you who stayed with the story to its conclusion.

First: thank you for giving your precious time to this work. That means a great deal to me.

When I was writing, I was too focused on getting each episode out to think much about the whole. Reading it back now, I'm struck by how rough the prose is — sentences that feel clumsy, places where I wish I'd found a better expression, things I'd do differently.

Regrets, yes. But the stars and hearts and reviews and encouragement from readers were a genuine source of strength.

The fact that I managed to post through to the end — I owe that entirely to the people who responded to this work.

Thank you, sincerely.

Regrets and all, I'm satisfied to have written Hiito and Seriha's story through to its end. Everything I wanted to say, I said.

This story ends here — but I'm currently writing my next work. (Also pure yuri, one hundred percent.)

I'm about seventy percent of the way through, so I expect to start posting in late April. (As with this one, I plan to post every day until completion.)

If you're curious about what comes next, I'd be glad if you followed me on X - [Rino_yr0](https://x.com/Rino_yr0), or on Kakuyomu - [Rino_yr0](https://kakuyomu.jp/users/Rino_yr0).

I hope you'll watch as the baton passes from Hiito and Seriha to the next pair.

Thank you so much for reading this far.

If you're interested in what follows — I would be very grateful for your continued company.
