## Original Title

学園のお姫様系女子が、王子様系女子を攻略しようとする話

## Description

### You know that type? The prince-type? They're always secretly the most feminine of all

---

Hanasaki Himena is a self-proclaimed princess-type girl who fell head over heels for Yaoji Iori at first sight and enrolled in the same high school to pursue her.\
She goes in convinced that cool prince-type girls are secretly the most feminine underneath — but the more time she spends with Iori, the deeper she falls.\
What starts as a conquest plan rapidly becomes uncontrollable infatuation on Himena's end, while Iori remains coolly unreadable.

"Just how far are you planning to make me fall for you, Iori-senpai?!"\
"I wouldn't know."\
"Ugh — and that expression is my absolute favourite thing about you!"\
"You're funny, Himena-chan."

A high-energy yuri rom-com featuring a pushy, princess-like younger girl and an older, prince-like girl!
